States

Rajasthan

Rajasthan is a state in northern India known for its rich history, vibrant culture, and stunning landscapes. It is the largest state in India by area and is often referred to as the "Land of Kings" due to its royal heritage. Jaipur, the state capital, is also known as the "Pink City" because of the pink-colored buildings in its old city area..

Kashmir

Kashmir, often referred to as "Paradise on Earth," is a region located in the northernmost part of India, known for its breathtaking landscapes, snow-capped mountains, pristine lakes, and lush valleys. It is a region of great natural beauty and cultural significance..

kerala

Kerala, located in the southwestern part of India, is often referred to as "God's Own Country" due to its natural beauty, serene backwaters, lush greenery, and coastal landscapes. It is known for its rich cultural heritage, Ayurvedic treatments, and diverse ecosystems.

Goa

Goa is a coastal state located in western India, known for its beautiful beaches, vibrant nightlife, rich cultural heritage, and colonial architecture. It is one of the smallest states in India but is immensely popular among domestic and international tourists.

UP

Uttar Pradesh is a large state in northern India known for its historical and cultural significance. It is home to several UNESCO World Heritage Sites, religious landmarks, diverse landscapes, and vibrant festivals.

National Capital Region

The National Capital Region (NCR) in India encompasses Delhi and its neighboring regions in the states of Haryana, Uttar Pradesh, and Rajasthan. It is the largest urban agglomeration in India and serves as the political, cultural, and economic hub of the country.
